Privacy Policy

At Ukhti Games, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share data when you interact with our hybrid casual games and our website.

Information We Collect

  • Device Information: Device type, OS, IP address, and unique identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Game sessions, levels played, scores, and interactions.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies to improve user experience and analyze performance.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve our games and services.
  • To personalize content and in-game offers.
  • To analyze usage trends and enhance game performance.
  • To communicate important updates or support messages.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with partners and analytics providers to improve gameplay and marketing efforts.

Third-Party Services

Our games may include third-party SDKs for ads and analytics. These providers have their own privacy policies and are responsible for the data they collect.

Children's Privacy

Our games are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
