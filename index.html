About the Founder

Ukhti Arcades was founded by Muhammad Touseef, a mobile game developer and playable ads specialist with several years of experience in the mobile gaming industry.

Touseef has worked with leading companies in the mobile gaming ecosystem including projects connected with major publishers and studios. His work focuses on building high‑performance interactive playable ads, rapid gameplay prototypes, and scalable Unity gameplay systems.

With experience across Unity, WebGL playables, Cocos, and advertising platforms, Ukhti Arcades focuses on creating engaging hybrid‑casual experiences designed for the global mobile market.

This studio was created with the vision of building innovative mobile games while also delivering high‑quality playable advertising experiences for partners and publishers.
